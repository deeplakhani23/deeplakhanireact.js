Business man:-

A successful businessperson, often referred to as a "businessman," is an individual who excels in the world of commerce and entrepreneurship. They possess a keen sense of market trends, innovation, and risk management, allowing them to identify lucrative opportunities and build profitable ventures. A businessperson typically exhibits strong leadership skills, effective decision-making abilities, and a dedication to achieving financial success and sustainable growth for their company. They often engage in strategic networking, negotiation, and resource allocation to ensure the long-term viability and competitiveness of their business in a dynamic and competitive global economy.
